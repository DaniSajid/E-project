Why Galleria for Artists?

By uploading your art pieces on our online art gallery you can reach a much wider audience and increase your chances of generating sales. Fitoor not only gives you a platform to showcase your art but also markets it to increase your reach. We also have a dedicated customer service team that facilitates potential buyers of your art and makes the whole process of selling your art smooth and hassle-free. Not only this but Fitoor also delivers the artwork on your behalf to the buyer, collects the payment and transfers it to you in no time. Our logistics team works day and night to ensure your artworks reaches its destinations all over Pakistan. We bear the entire burden from finding potential buyers for you to delivering your artwork to those buyers and transferring your payment to you so that you can just relax and focus on creating.
